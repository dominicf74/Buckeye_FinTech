# Buckeye Fintech

Buckeye FinTech is The Ohio State University's premier student organization dedicated to advancing financial technology knowledge. We equip students with practical knowledge and hands-on experience to excel in this revolutionary field. Join us to explore the evolving world of FinTech through education, collaboration, and innovation. From technical workshops to industry speaker events, our diverse activities prepare you to succeed and lead in the FinTech ecosystem.


Learn more about our organization at this link:
(https://www.buckeyefintech.org/)

## Our Projects

At Buckeye FinTech, we provide a multitude of opportunities to learn about the expanding financial-technology space through collaborative projects that involve concepts from low-level programming to high level financial planning and analysis.

## Buckeye Coin

A collaborative cryptocurrency creation project managed by Buckeye Fintech, a new finance and technology focused club at The Ohio State University.

Uses the Solidity development platform and smart contracts based on the ETH and SOL blockchains.
